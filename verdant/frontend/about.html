What We Do

At Verdant, we bridge the gap between digital innovation and environmental responsibility by empowering individuals and organisations to build a more sustainable internet. Our intuitive tool allows users to simply input any website URL and receive a comprehensive audit based on well-defined ‚ÄúGreen‚Äù metrics. These include assessing performance efficiency and page load speed to ensure smoother, faster, and energy-saving user experiences; analysing content delivery optimisation by checking if images and videos are properly compressed and responsive to reduce unnecessary data transfer; and evaluating code efficiency by identifying unused JavaScript, CSS, and libraries that add digital weight and consume extra energy. Additionally, we examine server infrastructure and its energy source to understand its broader environmental impact. Our platform also estimates the digital carbon emissions produced by data transfer and computational demands, making these often invisible impacts clear and measurable. At the end of each audit, users receive a detailed report featuring their sustainability score along with practical, actionable recommendations such as implementing lazy loading to optimise load times, using modern image formats like WebP for improved compression, enhancing hosting strategies, and adopting green hosting solutions powered by renewable energy. Through these insights and solutions, we transform everyday digital choices into conscious actions that contribute to a greener, faster, and more sustainable online world.
